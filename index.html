Bugatti

Founded in 1909 by Ettore Bugatti in Molsheim, France, Bugatti is renowned for producing some of the world's most powerful and luxurious hypercars. The brand's legacy includes iconic models like the Type 35, Type 41 Royale, and the Type 57SC Atlantic. In the 1990s, the brand was setting new standards in performance and design.

THAR

The Mahindra Thar is an iconic Indian off-road SUV that pays homage to the classic Jeep CJ series. Introduced in 2010, it has gained popularity for its rugged design and off-road prowess. The latest generation offers modern amenities while retaining its adventurous spirit, making it a top choice for enthusiasts seeking adventure and versatility

BMW M4

The BMW M4 Competition is a high-performance coupe that combines aggressive styling with precision engineering. Powered by a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged inline-six engine producing 510 horsepower, it accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.5 seconds. With a top speed electronically limited to 250 km/h.

Range Rover

The Range Rover, introduced in 1970, is the flagship luxury SUV from Land Rover, epitomizing sophistication and performance. The 5th-generation Range Rover offers a refined design with a floating roofline and continuous waistline, complemented by meticulously crafted interiors. Available in both standard and long-wheelbase configurations.

Defender

The Land Rover Defender is a legendary British off-road vehicle renowned for its rugged durability and versatility. Originally introduced in 1983, it has evolved into a modern icon that seamlessly blends classic design with contemporary technology. The latest Defender models feature advanced off-road capabilities, including Terrain Response 2 technology,

G-Wagon

The Mercedes-Benz G-Class, commonly known as the G-Wagon, is a luxury SUV that originated as a military vehicle in 1979. Over the decades, it has evolved into a symbol of opulence and ruggedness. Notably, the G 63 AMG variant offers a blend of high performance and luxury, featuring a 4.0-liter V8 engine and a distinctive boxy design.
